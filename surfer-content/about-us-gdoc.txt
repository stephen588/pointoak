How We Work: Analyze. Optimize. Implement.
We follow a three-phase approach that brings structure and accountability to every engagement.
Analyze. We start by examining your current plan structure, fees, investments, and processes to identify where improvements will have the greatest impact. This isn't a surface-level review. We dig into the details so nothing gets overlooked.
Optimize. Based on our findings, we develop strategic recommendations on plan design, investments, and provider relationships. Every recommendation is tailored to your organization's goals, your participants' needs, and the regulatory landscape you're operating in.
Implement. We put the plan into action and provide ongoing monitoring to make sure your retirement plan keeps performing. Quarterly investment reviews, annual benchmarking studies, and regulatory updates keep you ahead of changes rather than reacting to them.
Every step is documented meticulously. If you ever face an audit, your Virtual 401(k) Fiduciary File contains a complete record of every process, procedure, and decision, built to demonstrate the prudent process the DOL expects.

Why Plan Sponsors Choose PointOak
Independence You Can Trust
As an independently owned and operated firm, we answer to one group: our clients. There's no parent company pushing proprietary products. No recordkeeper relationship influencing our advice. When we recommend a provider, an investment, or a plan design change, it's because it's the right move for you, not because it generates revenue for someone else.
Named Fiduciary Accountability
We don't just advise from the sidelines. Our advisors are registered and act as named fiduciaries under ERISA, serving as either a 3(21) co-fiduciary or 3(38) investment manager with every client. That means we share the weight of fiduciary responsibility directly. It's the strongest commitment an advisor can make to acting in your best interest.
Benchmarking That Stands Up to Scrutiny
Most benchmarking studies rely on average plan demographics. Ours don't. Our "Live Bid" benchmarking process uses your actual plan data to generate results, reviewing over 450 data points per bidder across fees, investments, and services. With more than 10,000 benchmarks completed annually, our provider and fee benchmarking gives you the ammunition to renegotiate better terms or make a confident provider switch.
Investment Expertise at Scale
Choosing from over 40,000 available funds isn't something you should navigate alone. Our proprietary 10-point pass/fail scoring system combines institutional measurement techniques with clear, accessible analytics. We evaluate investment performance across fund style, risk-adjusted returns, peer group rankings, manager tenure, and expense ratios, all reported quarterly so you always know where your plan stands.
A Process That Protects You
We apply a meticulous, technology-driven process developed over years of client engagement. As diligent stewards, we document everything we do with you. Your Virtual 401(k) Fiduciary File is a digital record of all processes and procedures, purpose-built to protect you if you ever face a DOL audit or investigation.
