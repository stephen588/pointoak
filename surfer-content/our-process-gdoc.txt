Phase 1: Analyze
We Start by Understanding Your Plan Inside and Out
Before we recommend a single change, we need to know exactly what you're working with. Our analysis phase is comprehensive, covering your plan's structure, fees, investments, provider relationships, and fiduciary processes.
Here's what we evaluate during this phase:
* Plan Design Review: We assess your current plan structure, including enrollment features, contribution formulas, vesting schedules, and eligibility provisions. Are your plan's design elements aligned with your company's goals and culture? We'll find out.

* Fee and Provider Benchmarking: You've certainly thought about cost. But how much is too much, and what are you getting for your money? We conduct a custom Live Bid benchmarking study that reviews over 450 data points per bidder, using your actual plan information rather than average demographics. This makes our analysis among the most accurate in the industry.

* Investment Lineup Evaluation: With more than 40,000 funds available, selecting the right investment menu is no small task. Our investment analysis methodology evaluates over $120 billion in retirement plan assets quarterly, measuring fund performance across multiple factors using our proprietary 10-point pass/fail scoring system.

* Fiduciary Process Assessment: Are your processes documented? Is your Investment Committee structured properly? Do you have a current Investment Policy Statement? We identify gaps in your fiduciary compliance and create a clear roadmap for closing them.

* Participant Outcomes Review: We look at participation rates, deferral levels, and retirement readiness metrics to understand how well your plan is actually serving your employees.

The result of this phase is a clear, detailed picture of where your plan stands and where the opportunities are. No guesswork. No assumptions. Just data.
________________


Phase 2: Optimize
We Build a Strategy Tailored to Your Plan
Once we understand your plan's current state, we develop specific, actionable recommendations designed to strengthen every aspect of your retirement program.
This isn't about making changes for the sake of change. It's about making the right changes for your organization and your employees.
   * Investment Menu Optimization: Based on our analysis, we recommend adjustments to your investment lineup to reduce costs, improve diversification, and align fund options with your participants' needs. Every recommendation is backed by our quarterly monitoring data and documented in your Investment Policy Statement.

   * Fee Negotiation and Provider Strategy: We help you uncover inefficiencies. And then we provide you with the ammunition to renegotiate better fees and better services, or to make a provider switch. Our benchmarking data gives you real leverage because it's based on live bids from competing providers, not industry averages.

   * Plan Design Enhancements: We help you move beyond a cookie-cutter plan. Whether it's implementing automatic enrollment, adding auto-escalation features, or restructuring your match formula, we tailor design recommendations to your specific company goals and workforce demographics.

   * Fiduciary Framework Development: We draft or update your Investment Policy Statement, establish or strengthen your Investment Committee structure, and build the governance framework that protects you. Acting as your 3(21) or 3(38) named fiduciary under ERISA, we share the fiduciary responsibility so you're not carrying it alone.

   * Target Date Fund Evaluation: If your plan uses TDFs (and most do), we conduct a multi-step suitability analysis to determine whether your current solution is the right fit for your participants, or whether a different approach would serve them better.

Every recommendation comes with clear documentation of our rationale, making your decisions defensible if you're ever audited.

Phase 3: Implement
We Put the Plan Into Action and Keep It on Track
Recommendations only matter if they're executed well. We work directly with your providers, recordkeepers, and third-party administrators to implement every change efficiently and accurately.
But implementation isn't the finish line. It's the starting point for ongoing monitoring and support.
      * Change Execution: We coordinate with all parties to implement investment changes, plan design updates, provider transitions, and fee renegotiations. You don't have to manage the logistics.

      * Quarterly Investment Monitoring: Your plan doesn't stand still, and neither do we. We monitor your investment lineup every quarter using institutional-grade analytics, flagging any funds that fall below our performance thresholds and providing specific recommendations when action is needed.

      * Annual Fiduciary Plan Review: Each year, we deliver a comprehensive review presentation covering your plan's performance, compliance status, fee competitiveness, and participant outcomes. This keeps you informed and ensures your fiduciary file stays current.

      * Regulatory Updates and Education: The DOL and ERISA landscape changes constantly. We stay on top of the ever-changing fiduciary rules and regulations so we can implement best practices to keep you in compliance. You'll never be caught off guard by a new requirement.

      * Employee Education Programs: A great plan means nothing if employees don't use it. Our licensed securities advisors lead interactive sessions covering retirement savings strategies, budgeting, and debt management to help your workforce take full advantage of the plan you've built.

      * Virtual 401(k) Fiduciary File: We document everything we do with you in a secure digital file. Every analysis, every recommendation, every committee meeting, every decision. If you're ever audited, your documentation is already in order.
