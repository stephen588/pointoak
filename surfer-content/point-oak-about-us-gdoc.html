About PointOak Retirement Advisors

Your retirement plan deserves more than a generalist who treats it like one more item on a long list of financial products. It deserves a team that has dedicated their entire practice to one thing: helping plan sponsors like you build better retirement outcomes for your company and your employees.

That's exactly what we do. And it's all we do.

Retirement Plan Specialists from Day One

PointOak Retirement Advisors was built on a simple premise: plan sponsors deserve advisors who are fully immersed in the retirement plan industry, not dabbling in it between wealth management clients and insurance products.

We've been advising plan sponsors and participants since the very first day of our practice. We didn't pivot into retirement plans when the market got crowded. We started here, and we've stayed here, because this work requires the kind of deep focus that generalists simply can't provide.

That focus shows up in everything we do. Over 90% of our revenue comes from working with corporate retirement plans. Our investment methodology is used to evaluate over $120 billion in retirement plan assets every quarter. We perform more than 10,000 benchmarks per year. These aren't aspirational numbers. They're the result of building an entire firm around one area of expertise.

What Drives Us

Our Mission: To help plan sponsors achieve successful retirement plan outcomes for their company and employees by serving as advocates for plan fiduciaries and protecting their best interests.

Our Approach: Analyze. Optimize. Implement.

We start by analyzing your current plan structure, fees, investments, and processes to identify where improvements will have the greatest impact. Then we optimize your plan through strategic recommendations on design, investments, and provider relationships. Finally, we implement changes and provide ongoing monitoring to make sure your plan keeps performing. Every step is documented meticulously to protect you from liability.

Why Plan Sponsors Choose PointOak

Independence You Can Trust

As an independently owned and operated firm, we answer to one group: our clients. There's no parent company pushing proprietary products. No corporate revenue targets influencing our recommendations. No pressure to sell you something you don't need. When we tell you your plan needs a change (or doesn't), that advice is driven solely by your best interest.

Named Fiduciary Protection

We don't just talk about fiduciary responsibility. We've been registered and acting as fiduciaries since day one. Every PointOak advisor can serve as a 3(21) or 3(38) named fiduciary under ERISA, which means we put our name on the line to help protect you from liability. That's not a service add-on. It's how we operate with every client.

Benchmarking That Goes Deeper

Most benchmarking uses average plan demographics and generic data. Ours doesn't. Our proprietary Live Bid benchmarking process reviews over 450 data points per bidder using your specific plan information. The result is data accuracy that can beat standard industry benchmarks, giving you real leverage to negotiate better fees, better services, or both.

Investment Analysis Built for Retirement Plans

With over 40,000 funds to choose from, building the right investment menu isn't simple. Our proprietary 10-point pass/fail scoring system measures fund performance across multiple factors, including fund style, risk-adjusted returns, peer group rankings, fund manager tenure, and expense ratios. We use this methodology to evaluate over $120 billion in retirement plan assets every quarter, combining sophisticated institutional measurement techniques with easy-to-understand analytics.

A Process That Protects You

We apply a meticulous, technology-driven process developed over years of client engagement experiences. As diligent stewards, we document everything we do with you. If you ever face an audit, your Virtual 401(k) Fiduciary File contains a complete record of every process, procedure, and decision, built to demonstrate the prudent process the DOL expects.

Our Leadership

PointOak is led by Darsh Makim, CRPC, AIF, whose vision shaped the firm's commitment to retirement plan specialization and fiduciary-first advising. Our consulting team brings deep experience in ERISA compliance, investment due diligence, plan design, and provider management, all focused exclusively on the retirement plan industry.

We're not a firm where your plan gets handed off to a junior associate after the pitch meeting. You work directly with experienced advisors who understand the fiduciary obligations impacting your organization and who are accountable to you personally.
