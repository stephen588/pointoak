How do we know if participants need education?

Several indicators signal education gaps: low participation rates, minimal contribution percentages, high hardship withdrawal activity, or failed nondiscrimination testing. If employees rarely access plan resources or express confusion about investment options, education can address these issues directly.

What's the difference between education and investment advice under ERISA?

DOL Interpretive Bulletin 96-1 establishes safe harbors for participant education versus fiduciary advice. Education covers general investment concepts, plan features, and retirement planning principles. Advice involves specific recommendations for individual circumstances. Our programs stay within education safe harbors while providing meaningful guidance that helps participants prepare for retirement.

Can education help us pass ADP/ACP testing?

Better participant education directly impacts nondiscrimination testing outcomes. When non-highly compensated employees understand plan benefits and enroll at higher rates, your testing results improve. We've seen education campaigns increase NHCE participation by double-digit percentages, creating margin in testing calculations.

What topics should participant education cover?

Effective programs address enrollment basics, contribution strategies, investment fundamentals, employer match optimization, and retirement income planning. We tailor content to your workforce, covering debt management and budgeting for younger workers while focusing on retirement security and Social Security coordination for those closer to becoming retirees. Your responsibility as a plan sponsor includes ensuring participants have the knowledge to make informed decisions.

How do you document education for our fiduciary file?

Every session generates comprehensive documentation including attendance records, topics covered, materials distributed, and participant questions addressed. This documentation demonstrates your prudent process and commitment to participant welfare. Your legal counsel and fiduciary auditors will find complete records that integrate with our fiduciary support services for audit protection.
